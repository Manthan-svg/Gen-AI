Corporate Global Solutions - Employee Handbook 2024
1. Introduction
Welcome to Corporate Global Solutions (CGS). This document outlines the key human resources policies, expectations, and benefits for all full-time employees.
2. Recruitment & Probation
Probation Period: All new hires undergo a mandatory probation period of 90 days.
Performance Review: A formal review will be conducted at the end of the 60th and 90th day.
Notice Period during Probation: Either party can terminate employment with a 7-day notice during this period.
3. Working Hours & Attendance
Standard Hours: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday.
Core Hours: All employees must be present during core hours (10:30 AM to 4:30 PM).
Remote Work: Employees are eligible for "Work from Home" for up to 2 days per week with prior manager approval.
4. Leave Policy
Annual Leave: Employees earn 21 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
Sick Leave: 10 days of paid sick leave are provided. A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.
Maternity Leave: Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.
Paternity Leave: Male employees are entitled to 15 days of paid paternity leave.
5. Employee Benefits
Health Insurance: CGS provides a comprehensive health plan covering the employee, spouse, and up to two children. The maximum coverage limit is $50,000 per annum.
Learning Budget: Every employee is eligible for an annual professional development reimbursement of up to $1,500.
Gym Membership: The company subsidizes 50% of monthly gym fees, up to $40 per month.
6. Code of Conduct
Confidentiality: Employees must not disclose proprietary company data or client information to third parties.
Conflict of Interest: Any outside employment or "freelancing" must be disclosed to the HR department via the "Conflict Disclosure Form."
Dress Code: Business Casual is the standard from Monday to Thursday. Fridays are "Casual Dress" days.
7. Termination of Employment
Resignation: After probation, a standard notice period of 60 days is required.
Relieving Letter: Provided only after the full handover of company assets (Laptop, Access Card, and Company Credit Card).
